Why Shared Entities Matter

The Global Opportunities Sleeve is the same legal entity, but it participates in two different fund structures.

In a rigid system, you'd need to duplicate this entity or create complex workarounds. With the flexible Entity Kit model, the same entity can appear in multiple hierarchies while maintaining its single identity for KYC, compliance, and reporting purposes.

The entity's "kit" (documents, products, coverage) can be configured independently at each location in the hierarchy if needed‚Äîor shared across both.
